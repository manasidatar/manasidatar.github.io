About

"Do not take life too seriously. You will not get out of it alive." - Elbert Hubbard

Professionally speaking...

My general research interests are in the areas of image analysis, visualization, artificial intelligence and other related fields that converge with traditional image processing methods. IMHO, the best way to drive research is through a structured approach - ask the right questions, seek to answer them with an appropriate choice of experiments, keen observations, deep and thought-provoking inferences. Most of these qualities are enhanced in the midst of an exemplary peer group and at present, I work with just that kind of team at Medical Imaging Technologies, Siemens Healthcare Technology Center, trying to transform research into practical applications.

Personally speaking...

*almost* Sunday's child not so bonnie, cheerfully blithe and rest of the blah ;)
I'm an optimist and enjoy many little things in life; love to travel, love the outdoors, follow a plethora of sports, will read almost anything but prefer science non-fiction :) Currently hooked on to photography...
