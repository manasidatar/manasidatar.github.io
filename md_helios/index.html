About

"Do not take life too seriously. You will not get out of it alive." - Elbert Hubbard

Personally speaking...

Born and raised in Mumbai (Bombay), India, I got a Bachelors degree in Computer Engineering from University of Mumbai and followed it up with a Masters degree in Computer Science from Utah State University. After working with the Imaging Technologies Lab at GE Global Research, Bangalore (India), I joined the PhD program at the University of Utah, where I spent 5 wonderful years working on statistical shape models at the SCI Institute with my advisor Dr. Ross Whitaker.

Professionally speaking...

My general research interests are in the areas of image analysis, visualization and other allied fields (e.g. data mining, machine learning) that converge with traditional image processing methods to provide better solutions to problems in this field. I recently joined the Imaging and Computer Vision (ICV) group @ Siemens Corporate Technology and look forward to working on projects related to medical image analysis.
