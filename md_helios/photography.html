Evolving albums

My camera travels with me, helps me capture a moment and in fact create memorable moments at times.

There is much to be learned about photography and most of it is best learned from experience, by experimenting with the lens. Each such lensperiment adds to 'camera skills' :) e.g. even if one visits the same place over and over, there is always something new to see! Travelling alone is never fun, but seeing new things or recognizing things seen before is a cerebral exercise to make it slightly more interesting - my favorite thing to do, specially on day flights :) I've been in places where mundane things just stuck out and wanted to be captured ;) and of course, they happened exactly when I had nothing better to do but click away!
