Lensperiments

"Buying a Nikon doesn't make you a photographer. It makes you a Nikon owner." - Anonymous

That is why I own Canon gear ;) Jokes apart, even though I do not pursue photography as an art, there is much to be learnt and most of it is best learnt from experience! This collection is the result of my experiments with the lens. There is no specific theme or pre-determined principle explored with any scene. Rather, each scene dictates a direction for my experience with the camera to stretch in :)

Top down perspective

"The traveler sees what he sees, the tourist sees what he has come to see." - G. K. Chesterton

Chesterton is bang on target with that quote! There is always something new to see... even if you visit the same place (like I have been... for a couple of years now :P). Travelling alone is never fun, but seeing new things or recognizing things seen before is a cerebral exercise to make it slightly more interesting. Of course, seeing new things never ceases... as you can see from this collection - my favorite thing to do, specially on day flights :)

Mundane stuff

"Sometimes I do get to places just when God's ready to have somebody click the shutter." - Ansel Adams

Belief in God aside, I've been in places where mundane things just stuck out and wanted to be captured ;) and of course... they happened exactly when I had nothing better to do!!!
